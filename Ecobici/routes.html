Conclusions & Recommendations

Based on an analysis of the usage plots above, we have offer 4 recommendations:

  1. Actvity in Colonia Del Valle appears slower than activity in Colonias Roma, Condesa, Juarez and Polanco. Despite the large concentration of stations in Col. Del Valle, they appear underutilized. We would recommend promoting the service more actively in that area.
  2. The heatmap demonstrates some Colonias that could be good candidates for expanding the network of Stations. Colonias Santa Maria la Ribera, Guerrero, and the western edge of Doctores all border heavy activity.
  3. On the otherhand, the heatmap demonstrates that other Colonias will not be good candidates for expansion until Colonia Del Valle sees heavier traffic. Colonias like San Pedro de los Pinos, Narvarte, Alamos, Postales and Portales are examples.
  4. A close look at station markers with heavy traffic indicate that metro stations and grocery stores are major drivers of ecobici traffic. They could represent good areas to promote the service in underutilized areas.
