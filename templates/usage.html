Ecobici Use


This project used a random sample of the total rides data that represents 1% of the full dataset. Therefore, number presented below are approximate projections of the sample subset.

Choose a year:

What we observed in this graph was the following:
  • Overall, most trips using Ecobici where made by men. Men make around three times more trips than women per year.
  • Ecobici use increased in both sexes after 2013.
  • After 2013, the trips made by men users rocketed, reaching 7M trips during 2017.
  • Ecobici use by women has always remained around 2M without having any significant change, throughout the years.
  • For both sexes, trip number plummeted during 2020, probably due to the Covid-19 confinement measures.

What we could see in this graph was the following:
  • We didn't observe any trends that could signal a change in Ecobici use depending on month, season or year.
  • As with the previous graph, women use Ecobici less than men, regardless of year or month.
  • Use throughout the year remains stable, except in 2020, where trips dropped after March.

The main problem we noticed is the lack of use of the Ecobici service by women and also the current decrease because of the pandemic. Because of this we recommend:
  • Market Ecobici as a covid-safe system of transport (due to it being outdoors, individual, etc).
  • Interview women on how could the service be better for them.
  • Target women in next Ecobici campaigns.
  • Improve bike and cycling road infrastructure to make it safer for women.
